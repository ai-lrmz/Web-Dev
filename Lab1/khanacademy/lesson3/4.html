Spaghetti Carbonara

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 400g
Pancetta 150g
Eggs 4 large
Parmesan Cheese 50g
Black Pepper To taste

Step 1: Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water according to package instructions.

Step 2: Fry the pancetta in a large pan until crispy.

Step 3: Beat the eggs in a bowl, then add the grated Parmesan cheese and a pinch of black pepper.

Step 4: Drain the pasta and mix with the pancetta. Then, add the egg mixture, stirring quickly to make a creamy sauce.

Step 5: Serve immediately with extra Parmesan and black pepper on top.

Source: BBC Good Food

Chocolate Cake

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 200g
Cocoa Powder 50g
Sugar 200g
Butter 100g
Eggs 2
Milk 150ml
Baking Powder 1 tsp

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F) and grease a round cake tin.

Step 2: Mix all the dry ingredients together in a large bowl.

Step 3: Beat the butter and sugar until light and fluffy, then add the eggs one at a time.

Step 4: Gradually add the dry ingredients and milk, stirring until smooth.

Step 5: Pour the mixture into the cake tin and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean.

Step 6: Let the cake cool before serving, optionally topping with icing or whipped cream.

Source: BBC Good Food
